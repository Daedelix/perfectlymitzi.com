Crazy in love at any age

When recently widowed art historian Claire meets a charismatic younger sculptor in Florence, she never expects her careful world to tilt on its axis. Drawn into a whirlwind of passion, laughter, and second chances, she discovers that desire and adventure do not retire—they only grow bolder.

Set against sun-drenched piazzas, velvet opera seats, and the timeless gaze of Michelangelo’s David, Perfectly Crazy is a fast, sexy, heartfelt story about risking your heart when you thought the best chapters were already written.
